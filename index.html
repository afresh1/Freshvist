Welcome to Freshvist

A Checkvist client for webOS

This app is a work in progress, see the support site for a roadmap, but I need people bugging me about finishing it and adding features. It has been going very slowly without feedback. If you have suggestions or comments, please let me know.

I am planning to make this support some sort of cached mode where it keeps a copy of your lists on-device and syncs changes with the server when it can, however that is a more difficult problem to solve so it is a fair ways down on the list.

I could really use some help from a graphics guru who can help improve the look and feel of the app and make a better looking icon.

Now click this preferences button and log into your Checkvist account.
